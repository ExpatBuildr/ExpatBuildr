Markets produce spreads everywhere &mdash; between currencies, between information and action, between what skilled labor costs in one geography versus another, between where capital is taxed and where it is not. Expat founders are uniquely positioned to capture multiple spreads simultaneously because they already sit at the intersection of several markets.

At **ExpatBuildr**, market arbitrage is the practice of identifying and capturing value in these gaps &mdash; turning the structural advantages of the expat founder position into permanent, growing wealth rather than just a higher savings rate.

## The Market Arbitrage Stack

### I. The Currency Layer
Strategy for founders earning in USD and spending in Southeast Asia &mdash; how to structure money across borders for maximum purchasing power.

### II. The Tax Layer
How the Foreign Earned Income Exclusion works for self-employed founders, and what it actually means for your effective tax rate.

### III. The Information Layer
How information asymmetry creates income opportunities that most founders never see &mdash; and how to invest from abroad without giving up access to good markets.
