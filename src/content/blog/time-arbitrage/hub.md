Time is the one asset that does not scale with income. Every founder has the same 24 hours. The ones who compound fastest are not working more &mdash; they are working on fewer things, with better systems, and with the right people handling everything else.

At **ExpatBuildr**, time arbitrage is the discipline of multiplying your output without multiplying your hours. For expat founders operating across time zones, the right systems, SOPs, and delegation stack turn the timezone gap into a compounding advantage.

---

## The Time Arbitrage Stack

### I. The Async Layer
Business operations that run without your constant presence. Documented workflows, clear ownership, and communication norms that do not require real-time availability.

### II. The Documentation Layer
If it is not written down, it cannot be delegated. We build the SOP frameworks that let a business run consistently regardless of who is executing the task.

### III. The Delegation Layer
Hiring and managing a virtual team from Southeast Asia &mdash; and using the time zone difference between your location and your clients as a workflow asset instead of a liability.
