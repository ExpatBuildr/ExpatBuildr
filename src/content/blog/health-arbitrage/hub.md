Most founders treat health as a personal matter separate from business. Expat founders learn quickly that it is not. Your cognitive performance, your energy, and your long-term capacity to build are directly tied to your health infrastructure &mdash; and that infrastructure is dramatically better and cheaper in Southeast Asia than most people expect.

At **ExpatBuildr**, health arbitrage means access to world-class healthcare, dental, fitness, and longevity infrastructure at a fraction of what it costs in the United States. Staying healthy is not a lifestyle choice for a founder &mdash; it is operational infrastructure.
